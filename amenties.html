AMENTIES

Multi-Purpose Hall

An ergonomically designed majestic, multi-purpose hall with excellent acoustic and brilliant lighting system and a seeding capacity of about 600, intended to be used for conducting examination and organizing seminars, debates, declamation contest, quiz competitions, poetic symposia and other cultural activities. The facility of indoor games like Badminton, Table tennis etc. has also been provided in this multipurpose hall.

Guest House

This polytechnic has well furnished guest room with good accommodation.

LRUC

The Learning Resources Utilization Center (LRUC) in this polytechnic facilitates importing of learning and training by using the latest instructional multimedia techniques like OHPs, DVPs, slide projectors, SLR photography cameras, VCRs, VCDs, video cameras, computers etc.

LIBRARY

The Institutes library has been equipped with about 20000 books covering technical, scientific and general subjects and most of these are latent editions and have been procured under the World Bank Project. Costly books are kept in the reference section to be used by the staff and students as per need. In the library about 2000 are the Book Bank books. And also subscribing for more than 60 periodicals and journals, besides newspapers.

NOTE:

An Identity cum Library Card is given to the every student when he or she join the polytechnic college in the first semester. The validity of this card is three years. The holder of identity card is entitle to draw two books for a period of 14 days from the Library. An overdue charge of 25 Paisa will be levied per book per day beyond the due date. The member shall be responsible for all the books drawn by him/ her. Books lost, defaced or injured in any way shall have to be replaced by the borrower. He should satisfy about the soundness of the book before issue. A sum of Rs. 50/- will be charged for the loss of identity card and fresh security..

Playground

Well laid out play fields for the games like cricket, hockey, football, basketball, volleyball and kabaddi etc. stand provided. Such facilities have been the reason behind the consecutively commendable performance by the students of this polytechnic in the inter-polytechnic sports and annual athletic meets.

BROADBAND

This Institute is having connectivity of Internet with a speed of 2MBPS along with VPN over broadband having connectivity of 2MBPS (through NME-ICT Scheme).

Staff colony

Aesthetically designed residential accommodation for all its employees. There is 51 staff quarters of different types viz. Type V -1, Type IV-14 ,Type III- 8, Type II-20, and Type I-8.

Hostel (Boys/Girls)

One girls and boys hostel is situated at the main campus of the institute .Boys hostel is under construction.
